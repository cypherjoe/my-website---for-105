Short Stories

While my professional legal background focuses on navigating complex global transactions and cross-border litigation, my creative writing explores the human narratives caught within rigid social systems. Through original short fiction like "Fire Is the Devil’s Only Friend" and "Have You Seen the Stars", I examine the intersections of personal grief, institutional pressure, and adolescent resilience. I am also proud to have my work slated for upcoming publication in Issue 47 of the Berkeley Fiction Review in Spring 2027.
