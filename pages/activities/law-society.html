Law Society

I founded and co-chaired the very first Law Society in CUHK-Shenzhen. I organized student-led initiatives that build a close-knit community among future lawyers: this includes talks, mentorship, and events that connect classroom ideas to practice. I liaised with law firms, law schools, and previous alum practicing law. As of April 2026, the Law Scoiety has held two professional school application talks (with speakers from HKU JD, CUHK JD, Peking School of Transnational Law, etc.), one "mock case" workshop, and one career talk with a panel of lawyers in the Greater Bay Area. I proudly served the Law Society alongside eight standing committee members. We now have 200+ members in both Undergraduate and Graduate levels.
